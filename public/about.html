About Mundial2026.app

Mundial2026.app is an independent fan-made simulator for the FIFA World Cup 2026. The tool helps football fans explore match predictions, group standings, knockout brackets, and possible tournament paths for all 48 national teams.

What The Simulator Does

The simulator lets users choose a team, predict or edit group-stage results, calculate standings, and generate the knockout bracket. It is designed for fans who want to test scenarios before and during the tournament.

The 2026 tournament format includes 48 teams, 12 groups, a Round of 32, and a full knockout stage leading to the final. Mundial2026.app turns that format into an interactive prediction experience.

Editorial Purpose

Our goal is to provide useful football information and a practical prediction tool. The site includes explanatory content about the World Cup format, host countries, match structure, and how group results affect the knockout stage.

Independence

Mundial2026.app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to FIFA or any official tournament organizer. Team names, tournament references, and schedule information are used for informational and fan-experience purposes.
