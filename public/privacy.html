Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how Mundial2026.app handles information when you use the World Cup 2026 simulator.

Information We Store Locally

The simulator may save your selected team, match predictions, standings, and bracket progress in your browser's local storage. This helps you continue your prediction without starting over. This local data stays on your device unless your browser or device settings remove it.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics to understand how visitors use the simulator, including page views, screen views, language selection, simulation events, and general engagement. These tools help us improve the product and identify errors.

Advertising And Cookies

If advertising is enabled after approval, third-party advertising partners such as Google may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. These technologies may collect information about your browser, device, visits, and interactions with ads.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use its services at Google's partner sites policy page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the information provided on the contact page.
