Privacy Policy / Política de Privacidad

Last updated / Última actualización: June 6, 2026

1. About This Site

FIFA 2026 World Cup Simulator (mundial2026.app) is a free, interactive web tool that lets you simulate the complete 2026 FIFA World Cup tournament using AI-powered match predictions. No account or registration is required to use the simulator.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). The data we process is limited to:

  • Analytics Anonymous usage events (e.g., match simulations started, pages viewed) via Firebase Analytics / Google Analytics.
  • Advertising Anonymized advertising data via Google AdSense, including cookies used to serve relevant ads.
  • Local Storage Your simulation progress (selected team, match results, standings) is saved in your browser's localStorage so you can resume later. This data never leaves your device.

3. Cookies

This site uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics / Firebase): Help us understand how users interact with the simulator — which features are used, how many simulations are run, etc. This data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used by Google to serve advertisements relevant to your interests. Google may use your browsing history across sites to personalize ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com and manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process data independently:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API — Used to generate AI match predictions. Match data (team names, stage) is sent to the API; no personal data is transmitted.

5. Data Retention

Your simulation state is stored in your browser's localStorage and remains until you clear your browser data or click "Restart Simulation" in the app. Analytics data is retained by Google per their standard retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws to access, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Since we collect no PII, most requests can be fulfilled by clearing your browser's local storage and cookies.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at: mundial2026app@gmail.com
